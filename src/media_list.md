## Games

### _Jusant_ by Don't Nod

Website: https://dont-nod.com/en/games/jusant/

Finished: 5/2/26

Stars: [5/5]

Review: _Jusant_ does only a few things, but it does them remarkably well. The climbing mechanics are the obvious centerpiece of the game, and they feel halfway between Assassin's Creed and Grow Home. The climbing rope mechanics in particular are both a pleasure and a challenge. The game is also quite nice to look at, and even when momentarily stumped by a climbing puzzle, one only need stop and look around to regain some composure. I like all kinds of games, but this is my cozy place.
